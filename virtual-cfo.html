Virtual CFO Services

CFO-level financial strategy for startups and growing businesses -- without the cost of a full-time hire. We bring the financial leadership your business needs to scale with confidence.

Who Needs a Virtual CFO

A Virtual CFO is not a luxury reserved for large corporations. If your business has outgrown basic accounting but is not ready for a full-time CFO, this service is built for you.

Startups Post-Series A

Investor expectations demand structured financial reporting, burn-rate tracking, and runway analysis. A Virtual CFO ensures your financial house is in order from the start.

SMEs with Rs. 1-50 Crore Turnover

At this scale, financial complexity increases significantly. You need MIS reports, working capital management, and proactive tax planning that a bookkeeper cannot provide.

Companies Preparing for Audit or Funding

Whether you are facing a statutory audit, due diligence from investors, or a bank loan appraisal, a Virtual CFO prepares your financials to withstand scrutiny.

Businesses Scaling Operations

Expanding into new markets, adding product lines, or hiring aggressively all require financial modelling and forecasting that keeps growth sustainable and funded.

How It Works

A structured three-step process that gets your financial operations running smoothly within weeks, not months.

1

Assessment

We review your current financial setup, identify gaps in reporting and compliance, and define the scope of engagement based on your specific needs and growth stage.

2

Onboarding

We set up reporting frameworks, integrate with your accounting software, establish a compliance calendar, and align with your team on deliverables and timelines.

3

Ongoing Monthly Engagement

Regular MIS delivery, monthly review calls, strategic advisory, compliance tracking, and on-demand support for investor meetings, audits, or banking requirements.
